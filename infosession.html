Data_Analytics@GAC Info Session

Wednesday September 21, 2016

Interested in learning about data analytics? Please join us as the Information Technology Bureau and the newly formed Results and Delivery Unitare hosting a one day Data_Analytics@GAC info session.

Register now

A Government Priority

Raise your awareness around data analytics and its role in helping meet Government of Canada priorities. As part of the agenda, you will be introduced to some basics of data analytics, as well as learn about use cases from across the department. Finally, you will participate in an activity to pre-select projects for a formal Data Analyst Training curriculum currently being designed.

→ Learn more about Data Analytics

Goals

  1. Build awareness around data analytics and its role in helping meet Government priorities
  2. Pre-select projects and candidates for a formal data analyst training curriculum
  3. Initial building block for a Global Affairs Canada community of practice on data analytics
  4. Enhance awareness of departmental data and tool access among attendees
