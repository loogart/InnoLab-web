Get inspired

We have a design space to help you brainstorm your digitally innovative ideas! Located at 200 Place du Portage, our space is designed for collaboration. Within the space you will find erasable walls, technology (of course!) and marvellous furniture to name a few. As a general rule, our design space can accommodate up to 10 people.

Want to book it?

If you are working on a digital innovation and would like to use the room, or would simply like to make a quick visit, hit Book the Lab and fill out the three (3) question form!
